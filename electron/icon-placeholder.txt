Place your icon files here:
  icon.png   — 512x512 PNG  (Linux + fallback)
  icon.ico   — Windows ICO  (multi-size: 16,32,48,256)
  icon.icns  — macOS ICNS

You can convert a PNG to ICO/ICNS using:
  https://www.icoconverter.com/
  https://cloudconvert.com/png-to-icns

Or generate all three from one PNG with electron-icon-builder:
  npx electron-icon-builder --input=icon.png --output=./
